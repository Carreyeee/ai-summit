About the Summit

Where Practical AI Meets Real Business Growth

AI Application Summit 2026 Sydney is a premium, application-led AI business summit designed for organisations seeking to understand, evaluate and adopt AI for commercial growth.

The Summit brings together business leaders, SMEs, professionals, investors, AI solution providers, innovators and strategic partners to exchange executive insights, showcase market-ready solutions and build high-value business connections.

Unlike broad technology conferences, the Summit focuses on business application — how AI can improve productivity, sales, customer engagement, operations, workforce performance and business transformation.

Why Now

AI adoption is no longer optional — it's a competitive advantage

Businesses that apply AI effectively are improving efficiency, enhancing customer service, reducing operational costs and unlocking new growth opportunities. The challenge is no longer whether AI matters — it's knowing how to turn AI interest into practical business direction.

Spend one focused business day to save months of scattered research, vendor confusion and missed opportunities.

Key questions businesses are asking

  • How can AI help my business?
  • Which AI tools are relevant to us?
  • How do we apply AI without wasting time or money?
  • How can AI improve sales, marketing, operations, finance, HR and customer service?
  • How do we manage AI risk, privacy and governance?
