About

Not a tools conference. A summit for the whole AI economy.

AI Summit Sydney 2026 is a flagship business, technology and investment summit for the leaders building, funding, regulating and adopting AI — connecting policy, energy, compute, applications, smart hardware and capital across Australia and the Asia-Pacific.

Where policy meets industry, infrastructure meets innovation, and technology meets capital.

Programme Framework

Six layers of the AI ecosystem.

One day, the full value chain — from how AI is governed and powered, to how it's built, applied, embodied and funded.

01

Governance & Policy

Regulation, data governance, cybersecurity, responsible AI and trust.

02

Energy

Renewables, storage, grid reliability and the power demand behind data centres.

03

Infrastructure & Compute

Data centres, GPU cloud, sovereign AI and the infrastructure that scales AI.

04

AI Applications

Real use cases across healthcare, finance, property, enterprise and automation.

05

Smart Technology

Robotics, drones, autonomous vehicles, connected devices and intelligent hardware.

06

Capital & Investment

Venture capital, private equity, family offices, startups and commercialisation.
